Professional Profile

I am Luisito Jr. Bataller Labita, a Bachelor of Science in Information Technology graduate with over fifteen years of professional experience in Information Technology, Systems Administration, Network Infrastructure, Technical Support, Leadership, Business Operations, and Customer Service.

My career began in financial services, where I developed strong customer service, leadership, and operational management skills. As my experience grew, I transitioned into enterprise Information Technology, where I specialized in systems administration, network infrastructure, technical support, hardware maintenance, software deployment, and IT operations.

In 2023, I founded LMF Payment Services, a community-based payment and remittance center serving Los Baños, Laguna. Today, I continue to combine technology, business, and customer service to deliver dependable solutions to organizations and communities.
